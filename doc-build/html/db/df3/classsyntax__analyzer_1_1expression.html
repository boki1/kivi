◆ m_operands

std::vector<expression> syntax_analyzer::expression::m_operands
private

The operands associated with the concrete expression.

In the operands of the expressions are contained all associated expressions which are required in order to successfully process "this" expression. Important note is that the supported statements are also represented as expression having (if one is present) their specific peculiarity stored in specific way.
